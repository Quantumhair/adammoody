Home Base

Ithaca is
gorges.

Most of the year you'll find me in Ithaca, NY — where the trails are endless, the coffee shops are warm, and the weather keeps things interesting.

The Finger Lakes region has a way of getting under your skin. Dramatic gorges, world-class wineries, and a community of curious, creative people make it one of those places you don't just visit — you put down roots.

When I'm not in Ithaca, my wife Jeney and I are out exploring — another US city visiting friends and family, or somewhere further afield. Balance between the desk and the road is the real lifestyle hack.
